Summary

Enliko is an educational platform for cryptocurrency market analysis. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services and do not sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Enliko ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational cryptocurrency trading tools platform, including our website, mobile applications, and Telegram bot (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Telegram user ID, username, language preference
  • API Credentials: Exchange API keys (encrypted, stored securely)
  • Configuration Data: Strategy settings, risk parameters, preferences
  • Communication: Support messages, feedback

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Features used, actions taken, timestamps
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, app version
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your exchange account passwords
  • Your wallet private keys (we never store these)
  • Your real name or government ID
  • Your physical address
  • Your bank or credit card information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Service
  • Portfolio tracking and market analysis features
  • Sending service notifications and updates
  • Improving user experience
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All API keys are encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted via TLS 1.3
  • Access Control: Strict access controls for all systems
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Trading Logs: Retained for 90 days for performance analysis
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 30 days
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

6. Third-Party Services

Our Service may interact with:

  • Cryptocurrency Exchanges: Bybit, HyperLiquid (via your API keys)
  • Telegram: For bot functionality
  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS for hosting

We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using the Service at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us via Telegram support or email.

8. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analytics (understanding how you use the Service)

See our Cookie Policy for more details.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification via the Service
